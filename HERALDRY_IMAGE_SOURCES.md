# Heraldry Image Sources for Flog Article

## Open Source / Public Domain Image Sources

### 1. Wikimedia Commons - Heraldry Collections
**URL:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Heraldry
- **License:** Public Domain / Creative Commons
- **Notes:** Extensive collection of medieval heraldry, coats of arms, heraldic symbols
- **Subcategories to explore:**
  - Category:Coats of arms
  - Category:Objects in heraldry
  - Category:Heraldic crests
  - Category:Medieval art

### 2. Traceable Heraldic Art
**URL:** https://heraldicart.org/
- **License:** Creative Commons / Public Domain
- **Notes:** Curated collection from medieval sources with attribution
- **Specific collections:**
  - Wikimedia Commons Public Domain subset: https://heraldicart.org/wikimedia-commons/
  - Artists: AceDouble, AnonMoos, Care, F.rodrigo, Fry1989, Madden, Mouagip, Orem, Poznaniak

### 3. FreeVector.com - Medieval Heraldry
**URL:** https://www.freevector.com/medieval-heraldry-vectors
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
- **Notes:** Vector format (AI/EPS) - includes shields, crowns, griffins, eagles, lions
- **Attribution:** caponehun and other artists

### 4. National Archives of Sweden (Riksarkivet)
**URL:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Commons:Riksarkivet
- **License:** Public Domain / Free License
- **Notes:** Official Swedish heraldic drawings
- **Artist:** Vladimir A. Sagerlund (municipality coats of arms)
